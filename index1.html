Vision Of Department


To become the center of excellence and the aspiration in the discipline of Computer Science , Cyber Security , Artificial Intelligence by producing highly skilled professionals,
who with their analytical capabilities and proficiencies apply the technical knowledge for socio-economic development.


Mission Of Department


To provide a technically sound ambiance of learning and realizing the frequently changing traits of the software industry to pursue sustainable socio-
economic growth with the sense of ethics, professionalism and leadership to serve the community and humanity at large.


Chairman's Message


Dr. Fida Hussain Khoso

It gives me immense pleasure to invite you to the Website of CS Department, DUET. CS Department used to conduct its programs in conjunction with the Department of Computer Systems Engineering untill 2020, after which the Department of CS got its own building. The department offers Bachelors of Science (B.S) degrees in Computer Science , Cyber Security & Artificial Intelligence. Our graduates are already serving in the reputable public and private sector Software organizations both locally and internationally. The department provides a congenial environment for carrying out edge-cutting research and experimental work with modern state-of-the-art laboratories in collaboration with our top-notch faculty members. Our faculty is highly qualified and always ready to guide the students in any academic related problem, where as the courteous administrative staff is always attentive to address the students' problems. Please explore the department of Computer Science, I am sure you will love to be a part of this department. Dr. Fida Hussain Khoso Chairman
