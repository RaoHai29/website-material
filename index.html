Vision Of Department


To become the center of excellence and the aspiration in the discipline of Computer Science , Cyber Security , Artificial Intelligence by producing highly skilled professionals,
who with their analytical capabilities and proficiencies apply the technical knowledge for socio-economic development.


Mission Of Department


To provide a technically sound ambiance of learning and realizing the frequently changing traits of the software industry to pursue sustainable socio-
economic growth with the sense of ethics, professionalism and leadership to serve the community and humanity at large.


Chairman's Message



Program's Info


  1. Impart in-depth understanding of Computer Science fields according to international standards.
  2. Convert understanding to innovations.
  3. Build diverse careers in Computer Science as productive Computer professionals and technopreneurs for the socio-economic development of the society.
  4. Prepare students for the graduate level studies and research.
  5. To develop effective oral and written communication skills in students.
  6. Impart professional ethics and collaborative team player abilities.
  7. To prepare students for jobs/higher studies in computer science and related fields.
  8. To make students effective member of society.
