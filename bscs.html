Introduction of Computer Science

Mission Statement- Department of Computer Science:

Provide quality education and produce highly skilled professionals in areas of Computer Science and Information Technology. The graduates are proficient at solving the local and global research and development problems effectively with social responsibility. The department stands committed to create a strong relationship with industry and society to address contemporary and future challenges.

Computer labs

We have Ten (10) computer labs which are equipped with 400 high specs desktop computers. These computers include Corei7, Corei5 computers. All computers are centrally connected on domain based network. These labs provide the facilities of internet, intranet, individuals email address, individual secure profiles and separate storage areas.
